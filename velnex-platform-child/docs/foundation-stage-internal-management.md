## 5. First Strategic Step - Business Case Studies

Before approaching investment seriously, Velnex should prepare strong business case studies.

These case studies must prove:

- what problems small and mid-sized businesses face
- how Velnex identifies operational gaps
- how Velnex can create structured improvement plans
- how Velnex can coordinate vendors, specialists, and internal teams
- how Velnex can become a long-term management and operations partner
- whether the business problem is real enough for Velnex to solve
- whether the solution can become a payable service
- whether the model can later support management participation, profit share, or shareholding discussion

The case studies should be practical, real-business focused, and useful for investor presentation.
